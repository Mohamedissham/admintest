Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Transfer times vary depending on the destination and payment method. Most transfers to bank accounts are completed within 1-2 business days, while cash pickup and mobile wallet transfers are often available within minutes. You'll receive a notification once the transfer is complete.

You'll need to provide:

  • Your valid government-issued ID
  • Recipient's full name as it appears on their ID
  • Recipient's contact information
  • For bank transfers: recipient's account details
  • For cash pickup: destination country and city
